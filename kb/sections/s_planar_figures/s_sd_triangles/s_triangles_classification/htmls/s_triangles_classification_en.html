Definition. Triangle - is a geometric shape, which consist of three points, don't lying on one staraight line, three segments, two by two connecting these points, and also parts of plane, imited these segments.

Classification of triangles

Dependence on angles:

  • Acute (all angles of triangle are acute);

  • Right (one angle of triangle is right);

  • Obtuse (one angle triangle obtuse).

Dependence on length ratio of sides:

  • Scalene (all sides triangle are different);

  • Isosceles (two side triangle is congruence);

  • Equilateral (all sides triangle are congruence).
